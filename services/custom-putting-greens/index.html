How It Works

Turf Installed in 4 Simple Steps

From your first call to enjoying your new lawn — we handle everything.

1

Design Consultation & Layout

We meet with you to plan the green's shape, size, number of holes, and any fringe or rough zones. We work from your sketch or design one together based on your yard dimensions and desired playing experience.

2

Excavation & Contoured Base

We excavate to the required depth and shape the subsurface to introduce subtle breaks and slopes — the contours that make the green actually fun to putt on. A compacted crushed stone drainage base is installed before any turf goes down.

3

Putting Surface & Cup Installation

Stimp-rated putting green turf is cut and fitted to the contoured surface. Cup sleeves are set flush, and fringe turf (if specified) is installed at a different pile height to replicate the transition from green to apron.

4

Final Tuning & Walkthrough

We adjust cup positioning, confirm the roll on each section, power-brush the surface, and hand the green over with a demonstration. You'll know exactly how to maintain it and what to expect through the seasons.

Common Questions

Custom Putting Greens — Frequently Asked Questions

How realistic is the putting experience on an artificial green?
Using stimp-rated turf and a proper compacted base, our greens deliver consistent, realistic roll comparable to club greens in the Niagara Region.
Can I add a fringe or rough area around my putting green?
Absolutely. We commonly install multi-zone setups with a chipping fringe and rough perimeter — just let us know your layout preferences.
What maintenance does a putting green require?
Very minimal — occasional brushing to keep the fibres upright and a hose rinse after debris accumulation. No special treatments needed.
