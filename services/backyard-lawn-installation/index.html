How It Works

Turf Installed in 4 Simple Steps

From your first call to enjoying your new lawn — we handle everything.

1

Free Backyard Assessment

We visit your property, measure the space, assess existing drainage and surface conditions, and walk you through your turf options. You'll receive a written fixed-price quote within 24 hours.

2

Turf Selection & Scheduling

Choose from our range of residential turf products — pet-friendly, child-safe, or premium landscape options. We schedule your install date at your convenience.

3

Site Prep & Excavation

We remove existing grass and soil to the required depth, compact a crushed stone drainage base, and level the surface. This is the step most installers skip — we don't.

4

Installation & Walkthrough

Turf is laid, cut to fit, secured at edges, infill applied, and the surface is power-brushed upright. We walk you through the finished yard and care instructions before leaving.

Common Questions

Backyard Lawn Installation — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does backyard astro turf installation cost in St. Catharines?
Backyard installs in St. Catharines typically range from $12–$20 per square foot installed, depending on yard size, site prep requirements, and turf grade. We provide free written quotes with no surprises.
How long does the installation take for a typical St. Catharines backyard?
Most standard residential backyards are completed in 1–2 days. Larger or more complex projects — such as those with significant grading — may take 3 days.
Do I need to remove existing grass before you arrive?
No — we handle all removal and disposal of existing grass and soil as part of our installation process.
