Pricing & Quotes

How long does astro turf installation take in St. Catharines?
Most residential backyard installs in St. Catharines are completed in 1–2 days depending on size and site conditions. We handle all prep work including removal of existing grass and the drainage layer.
Is astro turf safe for kids and pets?
Yes. All turf products we use are non-toxic, lead-free, and certified safe for children and animals. We offer cooling organic infill options that perform well in Niagara Region summers.
How much does astro turf cost in St. Catharines?
Most residential backyard installs range from $12 to $20 per square foot fully installed, depending on the product grade and site complexity. We provide free written quotes so there are no surprises.
Do you offer financing for larger turf projects?
We don't offer in-house financing, but many of our clients use home equity or renovation lines of credit. We provide detailed written quotes you can submit directly to your lender.

Installation Process

Does astro turf require regular maintenance?
Very little. An occasional rinse with a garden hose and a light brush to keep the blades upright is all it needs. No mowing, fertilizing, or watering required.
What's included in the free estimate?
We visit your property, take measurements, assess site conditions, and provide a written fixed-price quote. The price we quote is the price you pay — no change orders.
How do you prepare the site before installation?
We remove existing grass and vegetation, excavate 3–4 inches, lay a compacted crushed stone base for drainage, then install a weed barrier before the turf goes down.
Do I need to be home during the installation?
Not necessarily. We just need access to your yard. Many of our clients go to work and return to a finished installation.

Materials & Quality

Do you offer a warranty on your St. Catharines installations?
Yes. Every Twelve Mile Turf installation comes with a 10-year workmanship warranty. The turf products themselves typically carry a 15-year manufacturer warranty.
How long does astro turf last in Ontario's climate?
Quality turf installed correctly typically lasts 15–20 years. Our products are UV-stabilized and tested for Ontario's freeze-thaw cycle. We back every install with a 10-year workmanship warranty.
What type of turf products do you use?
We use commercial-grade polyethylene turf from Canadian and US manufacturers — not cheap imports. All products are UV-stabilized and chosen for Ontario's climate.
Does astro turf get hot in summer?
Standard turf can warm up in direct afternoon sun. We address this with cooling infill options and advise on shade structures for areas with heavy sun exposure.
