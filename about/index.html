Who We Are

Our Story

Twelve Mile Turf Co. was founded in 2015 by Tony Ferraro after over a decade in the Niagara Region's landscaping industry. Tony had spent years watching homeowners battle patchy lawns, rising water bills, and the never-ending Saturday mowing cycle — and he saw that artificial turf was transforming properties commercially while most homeowners still didn't have access to a quality local installer. He started small: one crew, one truck, and a commitment to doing the job right. Over the following years, the business grew entirely through referrals. Amanda Khatri joined in 2018 and built the client coordination process that Twelve Mile Turf is now known for — clear communication, scheduled follow-ups, and an estimate-to-install timeline that consistently beats the competition. Today the company serves the full Niagara Region, from Grimsby in the west to Wainfleet in the south. The approach hasn't changed since day one: use better materials than the other guys, never skip the drainage prep, and stand behind every install with a warranty you actually honour. Nearly every job we book comes from a past customer's recommendation — and we take that seriously.

Why Choose Twelve Mile Turf

Built to Last, Priced to Respect.

There's no shortage of turf installers advertising across the Niagara Region, and some will give you a lower quote than we will. We're not going to pretend otherwise. What we'll tell you is where that difference goes: into the materials, into the site prep, and into the warranty. We use commercial-grade polyethylene turf with proper drainage backing — not the cheap imports that arrive with a too-good-to-be-true price. Every installation starts with proper excavation and a compacted crushed stone drainage base. That step adds time and cost, but it's the difference between a lawn that performs for fifteen years and one that starts lifting and pooling after the second winter. Our crews have been with us for years. They're not seasonal labourers hired off a job board for busy season — they're the same people who installed your neighbour's backyard and the yard two streets over. When something needs attention under warranty, we come back. We've done it. Our reputation in this region is worth more to us than the cost of a return visit.
